In spring 2026, EMRYS will embark on a week-long walking and music tour across the Lake District mountains. The tour is funded by the ‘Principals Award for Innovation’ received by the trio’s drummer Sam, on graduating from Trinity Laban Conservatoire of Music and Dance. The tour will see EMRYS trek for 7 days across the expanse of the lake district, performing their music in local community spaces, schools, churches and village halls. Recordings from each concert will be curated to form a debut album.
